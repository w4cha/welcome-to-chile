The site:

Hi, my name is Lucas Folch and I'm from Chile. This page was done as part of a homework for the CS50 Harvard course called Introduction to computer science, where they ask the students to create a webpage with an open subject, so I decided to choose my home country as the theme for this project as a way to showcase what the place I was born and raised has to offer. I hope you enjoy what I have created here.
